# About Section Draft

I am a poet and prose writer from Kenya, currently submitting my thesis for my MALS Program.

## Creative Background
As a writer, I have worked with Prof. Vievee Francis as my advisor, who nurtured my practice. In my writing, I have learned to follow characters where they want or should go, not necessarily where I want them to go, which is instrumental in how I work with brands. I want to listen, I want to know what matters to people and why, and then I work to make sure that we are both hearing each other.

## Professional Approach
Currently, I am leveraging AI, like on this web app, to make sure me and clients are on the same page, so much that I execute what is needed. I have a specific preference for interactive marketing, whatever that may mean in different contexts, like using a zine, or doing voice overlays in post production. I want to create work that not only benefits the brands I work with but also in one way or another contributes positively to the lives of their users/clients.

## Values & Philosophy
I believe that even though we are all doing different things, we always have the circles within which we loop with others. I want to exist in these circles. Customer satisfaction is my utmost priority.
